About

Starting at an early age, Sandy Wu has been creating functional content with an artisitc emphasis. At around age 14, she started a YouTube channel, housing tutorials and update videos about polymer clay, that grew to a little over 400 subscribers at its height. With strong entrepreneural instincts, she monopolized her artistic skills in exchange for both proprietary game and real-life currency. Her current deviantArt consists of a growing fanbase of over 200 watchers.

Starting around fifth grade, Sandy began designing websites through third-party services, such as Webs, Weebly, and Wix. Although art is her passion, she chose to pursue computer science in an effort to creatively bridge the main ideals between science and art.

When she is not studying, programming, or drawing, Sandy enjoys hiking, meeting up with friends and learning Japanese.
