About

Starting at an early age, I have been creating functional content with an artisitc emphasis. At around age 14, I started a YouTube channel, housing tutorials and videos about polymer clay, that grew to a little over 400 subscribers at its height. With strong entrepreneural instincts, I monopolized my artistic skills in exchange for both proprietary game and real-life currency. My current deviantArt consists of a fanbase of over 190 watchers.

Starting around fifth grade, I began designing websites through third-party services, such as Webs, Weebly, and Wix. Although art is my passion, I chose to pursue computer science in an effort to creatively bridge the main ideals between science and art.

When I am not studying, programming, or drawing, I enjoy hiking, meeting up with friends and learning Japanese.
